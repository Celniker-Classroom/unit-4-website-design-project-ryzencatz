The game consists of a large, interconnected world with over 18 different destinations. The players often find themselves at the beginning of Dirtmouth when they enter the game and they end at the Black Egg Temple, completing a full circle and traversing through many challenging terrains in between.

/ so what's the best destination?

Many people say that the place with the best art style and bosses is the City of Tears, which is Hallownest's capital; however, I peacefully disagree. In my expert opinion, I consider Greenpath as the best destination spot, if you're looking somewhere to go in the game. First of all, it has the best animation, which should automatically make it the best place. Second, you get to meet Hornet for the first time, and the boss fight with Hornet is one of the best ones in the game. Hornet is the main boss in this level, but some other enemies include the massive moss charger and the Vengefly King, who is necessary to defeat to save Zote the Mighty.
