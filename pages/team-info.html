The Team

the masterminds behind the game

Originally created for a game jam, the three never expected Hollow Knight to have such success.

/ who?

Team Cherry! (aka Ari Gibson and William Pellen, with Christopher Larkin as the music person).

/ who??

// Ari Gibson.

Ari Nicholas Gibson is an 42-year-old Australian artist, animator, and video game designer who worked on the art and animation in Hollow Knight and its sequel, Silksong.

// William Pellen

William Pellen is also Australian and he's a video game designer and former web designer. Like Gibson, he's famous for his work on Hollow Knight and Silksong. He's also the co-founder and co-director of Team Cherry.

Christopher Larkin

Christopher James Larkin once again is Australian. He's a composer for vide games, film, and television, also best known for his work on Hollow Knight and Silksong. He laso made music for Pac-Man 256, Outfolded, and other things.
