Characters

All the wonderful NPCs.

Hollow Knight features over 50 non-player characters, including quest-givers, merchants, Nailmasters, and story-critical figures scattered across Hallownest, excluding standard enemies and bosses, cuz those aren't as important as the cute little NPCS

/ who? ... or what?

There are three main types of NPCS present: merchants, story, and nailmaster, which some would consider to be merchants as well, so I suppose that would make it into two categories.
