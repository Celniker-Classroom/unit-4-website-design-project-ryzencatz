The Knight

The savior.
The hero.

The Knight from Hollow Knight is a silent, enigmatic wanderer who emerges from the shadows to explore the fallen kingdom of Hallownest. Small and unassuming in appearance, it wields a nail as both weapon and tool, carving a path through haunting ruins filled with forgotten lore and lingering corruption. Despite its lack of voice or overt emotion, the Knight’s journey reveals a quiet resilience and purpose tied deeply to the kingdom’s fate. As it descend further into darkness, it uncovers truths about sacrifice, identity, and the fragile boundary between containment and ruin.
