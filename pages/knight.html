The Knight

The hero.

The Knight from Hollow Knight is a silent, enigmatic wanderer who emerges from the shadows to explore the fallen kingdom of Hallownest. Small and unassuming in appearance, it wields a nail as both weapon and tool, carving a path through haunting ruins filled with forgotten lore and lingering corruption. Despite its lack of voice or overt emotion, the Knight’s journey reveals a quiet resilience and purpose tied deeply to the kingdom’s fate. As it descend further into darkness, it uncovers truths about sacrifice, identity, and the fragile boundary between containment and ruin.

/ who?

The main character of Hollow Knight is the silent, playable protagonist known simply as the Knight. While players initially control this small, masked wanderer without knowing its history, the story reveals that the Knight is a Vessel—a being constructed from the Pale King’s lineage and the dark essence of the Void. Unlike the NPC "Hollow Knight," who was selected and raised to save the kingdom, the Knight was an accidental survivor of a mass abandonment in the Abyss. Driven by a silent purpose, the Knight returns to the ruins of Hallownest to navigate its dangers, gather power through Charms and spells, and ultimately decide the fate of the world by confronting the infection at its core.

/ what does bro do?

In Hollow Knight, the Knight's primary role is to explore the ruined kingdom of Hallownest to uncover its history and ultimately deal with the source of a magical infection. Mechanically, the Knight fights enemies using a sword-like weapon called a Nail and harnesses SOUL from defeated foes to either heal their own shell or cast powerful offensive spells. As a Metroidvania protagonist, the Knight also continuously gains new movement and combat abilities—such as dashing, wall-climbing, and double-jumping—which allow them to access previously unreachable areas. Their overarching goal is to locate and defeat the three Dreamers to break the seals on the Temple of the Black Egg, leading to a final confrontation that decides the fate of the entire kingdom.
