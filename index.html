Hollow Knight

A legendary game.

Hollow Knight is a game following a tiny beetle-like creature who has no name, but is frequently referred to as “the Knight.” The Knight ventures through the fallen kingdom of Hallownest, which once flourished with lively bugs, but was now in ruins after the bug became sick with “The Infection,” which is a disease that entered the bug’s minds and made them mindless and angry. Hallownest’s ruler, The Pale King, had attempted to lock away the plague in a temple, but it had managed to escape anyways. The Knight’s mission is to find and kill the three bugs called “The Dreamers,” who act as guardians for the temple. After these bugs are killed, the Knight can confront the source of the infection and end it.

/ who made it? origin story?

Hollow Knight is made by Team Cherry. It is based off of a game called “Hungry Knight” which the developers made for a game jam.

/ characters?

Well, obviously, there’s the Knight. There’s also The Dreamers, Hornet, and numerous other creatures found in the game, such as the False Knight, the Mantis Lords, etc.
